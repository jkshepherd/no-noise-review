St. James’ Dispatch is a quiet, focused journal built for weekly match reports. The goal is clarity over clutter: a single story, told with clean typography, strong hierarchy, and generous white space.

Every edition is designed to feel printable and archival. No ads, no feeds, no distractions — just the match, the patterns, and the details that matter.

This project is a tribute to black-and-white sports papers and the ritual of reading the match review the morning after. It is built to last, and to be re-read.
